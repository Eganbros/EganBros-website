Confined Space
Access Works

Our team is highly trained and equipped to handle the unique challenges associated with confined space environments.

How can we help?

We specialise in providing safe and efficient confined space access works for plumbing in Melbourne, Victoria. Our team is highly trained and equipped to handle the unique challenges associated with confined space environments, ensuring that all work is carried out to the highest safety and quality standards.

Safe Entry & Work Procedures

  • Strict adherence to all relevant safety regulations and standards, including the Australian Standard AS 2865 for Confined Spaces
  • Use of appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) and safety gear
  • Continuous monitoring of atmospheric conditions to ensure a safe working environment

Comprehensive Risk Assessment

  • Detailed evaluation of the confined space to identify potential hazards
  • Implementation of control measures to mitigate risks
  • Development of a site-specific safety plan

Plumbing Services

  • Installation, repair, and maintenance of plumbing systems in confined spaces such as tanks, pits, tunnels, and ducts
  • Leak detection and repair in hard-to-reach areas
  • Cleaning and unclogging of drains and pipes in confined spaces
  • Inspection and testing of plumbing systems using advanced equipment

Compliance and Documentation

  • Thorough documentation of all confined space entries and work performed
  • Compliance with Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) regulations and standards
  • Detailed reporting and record-keeping for audit and compliance purposes

Our dedication to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction ensures that all confined space access works are performed with the utmost care and precision.
