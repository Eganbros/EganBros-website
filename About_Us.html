About Us

Egan Brothers Plumbing & Electrical is a family-run business with deep roots in the community.

Passion & Commitment

Our journey began in 2013 under the name Village Property Services, a venture born out of a passion for providing exceptional plumbing and electrical solutions. The decision to rebrand to Egan Brothers Plumbing & Electrical was a natural progression, inspired by a legacy that dates back to the 1980s.

Our name honours the original business established by our father and uncles, who were pioneers in the industry. Their commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction laid the foundation for our values today. We carry forward their tradition with pride, ensuring that every project we undertake embodies the same dedication and integrity.

At Egan Brothers Plumbing & Electrical, we specialise in a comprehensive range of services, from routine maintenance and repairs to complex installations and emergency solutions. Our skilled team of professionals is equipped with the latest tools and technology, ensuring that we deliver efficient and reliable results every time.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and providing tailored solutions that exceed expectations. As a family business, we treat our clients like family, offering personalised service and a commitment to excellence that you can trust.

Join us on our journey as we continue to uphold the Egan family legacy, delivering top-notch plumbing and electrical services to our valued customers. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we look forward to serving you for many years to come.
