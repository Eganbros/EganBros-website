Car Charging Installation

Our team of certified electricians ensures that your EV charging station is installed safely, efficiently, and in compliance with all relevant regulations.

How can we help?

We are committed to supporting the transition to electric vehicles (EVs) by offering professional car charger installation services. Our team of certified electricians ensures that your EV charging station is installed safely, efficiently, and in compliance with all relevant regulations.

Consultation and Assessment

  • Expert advice on the best EV charging solutions for your needs
  • Assessment of your electrical system to determine suitability for a car charger
  • Detailed consultation to understand your requirements and preferences

Residential Car Charger Installations

  • Installation of home EV charging stations for convenient and efficient charging
  • Integration with your existing electrical system, ensuring safety and reliability
  • Installation of both Level 1 (standard) and Level 2 (fast) chargers

Commercial Car Charger Installations

  • Installation of EV charging stations for businesses, car parks, and public areas
  • Solutions tailored to accommodate multiple users and high usage
  • Options for networked charging stations with user access controls and payment systems

Customised Installation Solutions

  • Tailored installation solutions to meet the specific needs of your property
  • Coordination with manufacturers and suppliers to source the best equipment
  • Customised setups for unique installation environments

Safety and Compliance

  • Adherence to all relevant Australian standards and regulations for EV charger installations
  • Ensuring proper grounding and protection against electrical faults
  • Detailed testing and commissioning to ensure safe and effective operation

Ongoing Maintenance and Support

  • Regular maintenance to keep your EV charging station in optimal condition
  • Troubleshooting and repair services for any issues that arise
  • Upgrades and expansions to accommodate new EVs and increased usage

Transitioning to an electric vehicle is a smart and sustainable choice, and having a reliable car charger installed by professionals is essential.

Why Choose Us?

We provide top-quality car charger installation services, ensuring that your EV is always ready to go.

  • Experienced and qualified technicians
  • Use of advanced technology and equipment
  • Compliance with all safety standards
  • Transparent and detailed reporting
  • Prompt and reliable service
